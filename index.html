Journal Entries
Contra entries (Cash ↔ Bank transfers), hand loans given/received, and adjustments
When to use Journal Entries:
• Cash Withdrawal from Bank — Debit Cash, Credit Bank (type: Contra)
• Cash Deposited to Bank — Debit Bank, Credit Cash (type: Contra)
• Loan Given to member — Reduces Cash/Bank until repaid (type: Loan Given)
• Loan Received — Increases Cash/Bank as a liability (type: Loan Received)
• Loan Repaid — Restores balance when loan is returned (type: Loan Repaid)
